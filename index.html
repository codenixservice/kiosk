EdgeHub Manila

In-Person Visit Terms & Policies

Visit Duration Policy

Visitors are allowed to stay for a maximum of 1 hour per day at EdgeHub Manila for purposes such as learning, student projects, technical discussions, or interviews.

For casual or social visits, the maximum allowed duration is 30 minutes per day.

Requests to extend any visit beyond these limits must be approved in advance by an EdgeHub Manila administrator.

Overstaying without permission may result in restricted or permanent loss of future access.



Privacy Policy

All personal and behavioral data generated during your stay at EdgeHub Manila will be handled with the highest privacy standards.

We are committed to protecting your data in accordance with applicable laws, including RA 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012, Philippines).

You have the right to access, modify, or request deletion of your personal data collected within EdgeHub Manila premises.

Data is used strictly for security, coordination, and administrative purposes, and is not shared externally without consent.





EdgeHub Manila In-Person Visit Terms and Conditions

Effective Date: August 8, 2025

By scheduling or attending an in-person visit to EdgeHub Manila, you acknowledge and agree to comply with the following terms and conditions. These provisions are established to ensure a safe, secure, and respectful environment for all participants, staff, and equipment within the premises.





1. Entry and Access Control

1.1. All visits to EdgeHub Manila are strictly by prior appointment. Walk-ins or unscheduled visitors will not be admitted under any circumstance.

1.2. Visitors must present proof of their appointment upon entry and may be required to sign a visitor log or undergo identity verification.

1.3. EdgeHub Manila reserves the right to refuse or revoke access at any time, without obligation to provide justification.

1.4. Re-entry after exiting the premises is not permitted without a new scheduled appointment.





2. Use of Property and Conduct

2.1. Visitors shall not, under any circumstance, touch, use, operate, move, or interfere with any EdgeHub Manila equipment, devices, materials, or property without explicit prior authorization. This includes computers, servers, workstations, office equipment, tools, and furniture.

2.2. Access to computer systems, network resources, or restricted areas is strictly prohibited unless formally authorized in writing.

2.3. Visitors may be assigned designated seating. Unauthorized use of any desk, chair, or room is not allowed.

2.4. Recording (audio or video), photography, and any form of surveillance are prohibited unless written permission is granted in advance.





3. Personal Conduct and Hygiene

3.1. Visitors are required to maintain cleanliness and proper hygiene at all times, including in shared facilities such as restrooms.

3.2. Visitors must take all trash and belongings upon departure.

3.3. Food and beverages are not allowed without prior written approval. Items with strong or unpleasant odors are strictly prohibited.





4. Duration and Time Limits

4.1. Each visitor is permitted to stay for a maximum of one (1) hour per day unless prior written authorization has been obtained from EdgeHub Manila administration.

4.2. Requests for extended visits must be made in advance and are subject to approval.

4.3. Failure to comply with the designated time limit may result in immediate removal and permanent revocation of access privileges.





5. Electronic Devices and Power Access

5.1. Visitors intending to bring electronic equipment must declare all such devices during the registration process.

5.2. Electrical power will only be provided to pre-approved devices. Unauthorized usage of lab power sources is prohibited.





6. Damages and Liability

6.1. Visitors shall be held fully responsible for any damage caused to EdgeHub Manila property, and full monetary compensation will be required.

6.2. EdgeHub Manila assumes no responsibility for the loss, theft, or damage of personal items. Visitors are advised to monitor their belongings at all times.





7. Privacy and Data Protection

7.1. All personal data collected during your visit will be handled in accordance with the highest standards of confidentiality and in compliance with Republic Act No. 10173 (Data Privacy Act of 2012, Philippines).

7.2. Visitors have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data collected within EdgeHub Manila premises.

7.3. CCTV surveillance is in operation. By entering the premises, you consent to being recorded for safety and security purposes. Footage may be retained for up to thirty (30) days.





8. Enforcement and Penalties

8.1. Any violation of these terms may result in immediate expulsion and permanent revocation of visitation privileges.

8.2. EdgeHub Manila reserves the right to perform bag checks or conduct security screenings if deemed necessary.

8.3. All appointments are subject to cancellation or rescheduling without prior notice.





Acknowledgment

By proceeding with your appointment or entering the EdgeHub Manila premises, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to the above Terms and Conditions in full.
